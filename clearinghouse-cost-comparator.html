What would your clearinghouse cost if it were priced like water?

Legacy clearinghouses hide pricing behind enterprise sales. Stedi publishes per-transaction rates and holds to the tenet: "prices never go up, only down." This tool shows the delta, using your transaction mix plus public benchmarks from Availity, Change Healthcare, Waystar, Trizetto, Claim.MD, Anvicare, and pVerify.

What's not included

  • Payer-specific surcharges (some Blues plans, MA payers, certain TPAs) billed at cost on Stedi
  • RCM team labor savings from denial reduction material but customer-specific
  • Time-to-market savings from faster integration (hours vs weeks) material but hard to monetize
  • Risk mitigation value vs concentration at one legacy vendor Feb 2024 Change outage cost $2.87B industry-wide · AHA

How I'd harden this for production

  • Source rates from Stedi's internal pricing table (single source of truth) + a benchmark rate card maintained by Finance
  • Accept a CSV of last-90-day transaction volumes from Stedi's own billing system for existing-customer modeling
  • Embed into Salesforce as a Lightning component so AEs can run a comparison inside a deal
  • Log scenarios + actual closed deal outcomes to calibrate the model over time
