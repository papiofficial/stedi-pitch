How this scores — and how I'd improve it in production

›

Scoring model (simple additive, max 100)

  • Volume fit (max 25 pts) — How well the estimated volume aligns with Stedi's Developer-plan sweet spot (10K-5M/mo) vs. Enterprise (5M+).
  • ICP alignment (max 30 pts) — Company stage + fit signals (GenAI, API-first, multi-payer, burned by Change). Weighted from the Stedi customer case studies.
  • Competitive dynamics (max 15 pts) — Incumbent vendor + urgency. Change/Optum incumbents + urgent timeline = highest displacement probability.
  • Expansion & reference potential (max 20 pts) — Expansion path visible + willing-to-be-referenceable logo. These compound revenue impact beyond the initial deal.
  • Source quality (max 10 pts) — Warm intro > partner > event > website > outbound response. Higher source quality = faster cycle.

Tier routing logic

  • Basic (Free / PAYG) → pre-seed, <10K/mo volume, self-serve intent
  • Developer ($500/mo) → Seed/A, 10K-500K/mo, API-first, low complexity
  • Enterprise (Custom) → 500K+/mo, complex ask (BAA, SLA, custom integration), late-stage or incumbent replacement, multi-year potential

Owner routing logic

  • Self-serve / docs — Low-priority or simple ask. No human yet.
  • AE, small team — Medium priority. Standard discovery motion.
  • AE + Solutions Engineer — High priority with complexity flag. Need technical discovery.
  • CEO / VP Sales — Highest priority: strategic logo, competitive displacement, or reference-grade opportunity.

How I'd harden this for production

  • Pull signals automatically — Clearbit/Gong for company data; LinkedIn for contact title; Crunchbase for funding stage. Reduces manual entry to <5 fields.
  • Close the loop — Log every triage to Salesforce + tag closed-won/closed-lost outcome 90 days later. Retrain the scoring weights quarterly against actual win rate.
  • Deploy inside Salesforce — Lightning component or Slack slash command. No separate app, no context-switching for AEs.
  • Add a fast-track lane for Change-outage migrations — Automatically surface for CEO review; Stedi closed 6+7-figure deals in under an hour during the 2024 outage. Speed is the moat.
  • Instrument the cost of toil — If the same lead type scores consistently low but still gets routed to humans, that's automation oxygen.
